How to play


The rocket & Upgrades


The main mechanic in this game is the rocket, which you launch in order to gain Neta.
As long as it has been launched, the rocket passively generates Neta.
In the early game, the amount of neta is equal to the height of the rocket times the exchange rate.
The rocket will continue to generate neta until it runs out of fuel.
Once the rocket runs out of fuel, it will refuel at a rate proportional to it's maximum fuel.

The rocket also has four upgrades.
The first upgrade simply increases the exchange rate. At base, this is 1.3x per upgrade.
The second rocket increases base speed. This is at base 1.12x per upgrade.
Both of these upgrades also have an additive effect.
The third upgrade increases the amount of fuel the rocket has, at +5 per upgrade.
The fourth upgrade increases the maximum height the rocket can reach, at +0.1 km per upgrade.

Some important things to know about height is that for every power of 2 your height reaches, it slows down significantly.
However, after the first slowdown, height also gives a large multipler based on how far you are from the slowdown.


Augments


Augments are unlocked after reaching 1e10 neta. These are one-time purchases which use Tech Points as currency.
You unlock Tech Points by reaching new milestones of neta. At the beginning, Tech Points can be spent on two different "trees" - the Grinder tree and the Booster tree. The Grinder tree starts out weaker than the Booster tree, but grows in strength as you launch more. The Booster tree mostly gives flat boosts to production, remaining at the same strength throughout. Augments can also unlock additional upgrades, features, and more.
